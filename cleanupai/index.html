Cleanup AI

Smarter cleanup for media and selected Gmail clutter.

Cleanup AI helps users review duplicate photos, similar media, screenshots, large videos, and selected Gmail inbox items in a clean, guided workflow. Users stay in control of sign-in, review, and deletion actions at every step.

What Cleanup AI currently supports

  • On-device media review flows for duplicate and similar photos, screenshots, and large videos.
  • Optional Google sign-in to review selected Gmail inbox items.
  • Inbox grouping by sender, category, age, and attachment filters.
  • User-triggered Gmail trash actions for selected messages.
