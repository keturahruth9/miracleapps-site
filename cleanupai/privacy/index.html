Information you choose to use

Cleanup AI may request access to photos, videos, contacts, files, or Gmail only when those features are used. The app needs that access to find clutter, compress media, organize items, or show cleanup suggestions.

Media and file handling

Photo, video, and private-file actions are designed around review-first cleanup. You decide what to keep, compress, move, or delete.

Optional Gmail cleanup

If you use Gmail cleanup, Google may handle authentication and account access. We only use access for the cleanup feature you choose to run.

Purchases and ads

Some features may require Pro access or an in-app purchase. Purchases are handled by Apple or Google. Free users may see ads depending on the app version.

What we do not do

We do not sell your personal information. We do not ask for your full payment card details. We do not publish your private media or messages.

Contact
