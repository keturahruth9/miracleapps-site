Privacy Policy

📅 Effective Date: June 3, 2026

Welcome to CMA Part 2 Exam Preparation. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how the app and its companion pages handle data. In short, the app is designed to work without collecting or storing your personal information.

Section 01

📋 Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the CMA Part 2 Exam Preparation app and its related public information pages. It describes what data the app does not collect, how the app works offline, and what happens if you contact us directly by email.

Section 02

🛡️ Information We Do Not Collect

The CMA Part 2 Exam Preparation app does not collect, sell, or share personal data through its core study experience. We do not ask for:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or account credentials
  • IP addresses, device identifiers, or profile tracking data
  • Analytics events, advertising identifiers, or behavior tracking
  • Uploaded documents, photos, or any user-generated cloud content

Privacy-first by default: the app is intended to be usable without sign-in flows, marketing trackers, or hidden data collection of any kind.

Section 03

🔒 Permissions and Device Access

The app does not require access to your camera, location, contacts, microphone, or other sensitive device features to deliver its intended study functionality.

You can use the app without granting special permissions that are not directly required for normal study use. The only data processed locally are your quiz results, saved questions, and preferences — all stored on-device using encrypted secure storage.

Section 04

📴 Offline Use and Local Storage

The app is designed to operate as a fully offline study tool. All question banks, explanations, and features — including on-device ML translation — function without an internet connection.

Your quiz history, saved questions, exam mode preference, and exam date are stored locally on your device using encrypted secure storage. This information is never transmitted to us or any third party as part of normal app use.

You can permanently delete all locally stored data at any time via Settings → Clear History inside the app.

Section 05

🔗 Third-Party Services

The app may display ads in the free version through standard advertising platforms. Upgrading to Pro removes all ads. The on-device ML translation feature uses Google ML Kit, which processes text entirely on your device without sending data to external servers.

This website may be served through a standard static hosting platform, but the app itself does not include third-party services for user tracking or personal data monetization.

Section 06

✉️ Contacting Us

If you email us for support or feedback, we will receive the information you choose to include in that message — such as your email address and the contents of your request.

We use that information only to respond to your question or support request and do not store it in any marketing or analytics system.

Section 07

🔄 Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time for clarity, legal compliance, or product changes. If we do, the revised version will appear on this page with a new effective date. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
