CMA Part 2 Privacy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 3, 2026

Welcome to CMA Part 2 Exam Preparation. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how the app and its companion information pages handle data. In short, the app is designed to work without collecting or storing your personal information.

1. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the CMA Part 2 Exam Preparation app and its related public information pages. It describes what data the app does not collect, how the app works offline, and what happens if you contact us directly by email.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The CMA Part 2 Exam Preparation app does not collect, sell, or share personal data through its core study experience. We do not ask for:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or account credentials
  • IP addresses, device identifiers, or profile tracking data
  • Analytics events, advertising identifiers, or behavior tracking
  • Uploaded documents, photos, or any user-generated cloud content

Privacy-first by default: the app is intended to be usable without sign-in flows, marketing trackers, or hidden data collection.

3. Permissions and Device Access

The app does not require access to your camera, location, contacts, microphone, or other sensitive device features to deliver its intended study functionality.

You can use the app without granting special permissions that are not directly required for normal study use.

4. Offline Use and Local Storage

The app is designed to operate as an offline-friendly study tool. Since personal data is not collected by the core experience, there is no personal account database connected to your study activity.

If any app content is stored locally on your device for convenience, that information remains on your device and is not transmitted to us as part of normal use.

5. Third-Party Services

The app does not rely on advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or third-party data brokers to deliver the core study experience.

This website may be delivered through a standard static hosting platform, but the app itself does not include third-party services for user tracking or personal data monetization.

6. Contacting Us

If you email us for support or feedback, we will receive the information you choose to include in that message, such as your email address and the contents of your request.

We use that information only to respond to your question or support request.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time for clarity, legal compliance, or product changes. If we do, the revised version will appear on this page with a new effective date.
