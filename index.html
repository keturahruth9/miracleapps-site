About Cleanup AI

Cleanup AI helps users review duplicate photos, similar media, screenshots, large videos, and selected Gmail inbox items in a clean, guided flow. Features are designed to give users visibility before they take action, including review-first deletion flows and explicit sign-in for Gmail-based cleanup tools.
