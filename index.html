🤖 Who We Are

An AI-first studio.
Built for real life.

Miracle Apps is an independent AI-powered mobile product company based in India. We build apps where artificial intelligence does the heavy lifting — so users get smarter results with less effort.

We don't bolt AI on as a gimmick. It's the core of how our products work — from intelligently classifying photos to cleaning inboxes by pattern, our AI runs on your device, privately and instantly.

Privacy is non-negotiable. All AI processing happens on-device. Your data never leaves your phone.
