Privacy Policy

Your PMP study data should stay simple and protected.

This Privacy Policy explains how PMP Exam Preparation handles information when you use the app and related website. We keep the app focused on study progress, practice, and support.

Effective Date: June 6, 2026
PMP Exam Preparation does not require you to create an account to use the core app. Your quiz progress, saved questions, missed questions, exam mode, exam date, and Pro status are stored on your device where possible.

1. Information You Provide

You may choose to enter or use study-related information inside the app, such as exam date, preferred exam mode, saved questions, missed questions, and quiz progress.

If you email us for support, we receive your email address and the information you include in your message so we can reply and help you.

2. Local Study Data

The app stores study progress on your device using local/secure storage. This can include:

  • quiz results and answer history
  • readiness score progress
  • saved and missed questions
  • exam mode and exam date preferences
  • premium access status

If you uninstall the app, clear device storage, or reset your device, locally stored study data may be removed.

3. Purchases and Subscriptions

Some features may be available through in-app purchase, such as lifetime access, monthly access, ad removal, more questions, final quiz access, and advanced review tools.

Purchases are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play, depending on your device. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

4. Ads

Free users may see ads in the app. Ads may be served through Google Mobile Ads or similar ad services. These services may process device identifiers, ad interaction data, or related technical information according to their own policies.

If you upgrade to Pro and ads are removed, ad-related behavior inside the app is reduced or disabled where applicable.

5. Notifications and Reminders

The app may offer reminders to help you stay consistent with study practice. Notifications are controlled by your device settings, and you can turn them off at any time.

6. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not ask for your full payment card details.
  • We do not require a login account for normal study use.
  • We do not use your saved or missed questions for public posting.

7. Third-Party Services

The app may rely on platform and third-party services for purchases, ads, crash prevention, device storage, or browser links. Their handling of information is governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

8. Children's Privacy

PMP Exam Preparation is intended for exam preparation and professional study. It is not directed to children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us and we will review the request.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app, website, legal requirements, or third-party services change. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

10. Contact Us
