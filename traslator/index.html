Features

Built for everyday translation, not just one text box.

The app supports quick text translation, camera and gallery OCR, conversation translation, audio transcription, document translation, local history, and offline model downloads for supported languages.

Text translation

Type or paste text, choose source and target languages, translate, listen with text-to-speech, copy results, share results, and keep useful translations in history.

Photo and camera translation

Use the camera or pick an image from Photos. The app reads text from the image with OCR, translates it, and can create a translated image result.

Voice conversation translation

Speak into the microphone, convert speech to text, translate between two selected languages, and play translated speech when supported.

Audio transcription and translation

Choose audio files such as MP3, M4A, WAV, FLAC, OGG, MPEG, MPGA, or WEBM, then transcribe and translate the spoken content.

File translation

Translate PDFs, TXT files, DOCX documents, PPTX presentations, XLSX spreadsheets, and CSV files, then open or share the translated output.

Offline mode and Pro access

Download supported on-device translation models for offline use, manage settings, restore purchases, and unlock Pro features through in-app purchase.
