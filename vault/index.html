Features

Built for private media, quick access, and control.

Protect selected photos and videos behind a calculator-style lock, organize private albums, browse discreetly, and restore access when needed.

Calculator-style lock

Use a numeric passcode entry flow that looks like a calculator-style unlock screen before entering the vault.

Face ID or Touch ID

Turn biometric unlock on or off from Settings when your device supports Apple biometric authentication.

Photo and video vaults

Import photos and videos into app storage, browse thumbnails, preview images, play videos, select items, delete items, or restore selected files to Photos.

Captured images

Keep camera-captured images in a separate vault area so they stay organized apart from imported photos.

Built-in browser

Open a web browser inside the app with back and forward controls, page title display, and loading feedback.

CloudKit media sync

Vault media can sync through Apple's private CloudKit database using a Keychain-backed vault identifier and device metadata.
