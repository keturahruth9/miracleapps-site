Overview

Secret Vault Pro is designed to help you store private photos, videos, captured images, and browsing access behind a passcode and optional Face ID or Touch ID. This policy explains the data the app uses and how it is handled.

Permissions

The app may request access to your camera and photo library so you can add photos and videos to the vault or restore selected files back to Photos. You can manage these permissions in iOS Settings.

Local vault data

Your passcode, biometric setting, vault media, album counts, and app preferences may be stored locally on your device. Vault files are saved in the app's private storage area unless you choose to restore selected files to Photos or delete them.

Keychain and CloudKit

The app may create a vault identifier stored in Apple Keychain. Vault media and related metadata may sync through Apple's private CloudKit database using your iCloud account, including a vault identifier, device identifier, folder name, media type, file name, created date, and the media asset.

Ads

Secret Vault Pro may show Google Mobile Ads, including banner, native, app-open, and interstitial ads. Google Mobile Ads may process device identifiers, ad interaction data, and technical information according to Google's advertising policies.

Built-in browser

The app includes a built-in browser. Websites you open may collect information according to their own privacy policies. Miracle Apps does not control third-party websites or their privacy practices.

What we do not do

We do not sell your personal information, ask for your full payment card details, or access your photos and videos unless you grant the required iOS permissions or choose an app action that uses those files.

Children's privacy

Secret Vault Pro is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review the issue.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
